For more information on Alameda County's Restaurant grading policies, please see this F.A.Q. or this reviewer's handbook on the Alameda County website.

The different colors on the map mean the following:

Green: Score of 80-100 points with no more than one MAJOR CDC risk factor violation which is CORRECTED during inspection.
Yellow: Score of 75-79 points and/or two or more MAJOR CDC risk factor violations which are CORRECTED during inspection.
Red: A score of less than 75 points and/or one or more MAJOR CDC risk factor violation which is NOT CORRECTED during inspection or any Imminent Health Hazard see Cal Code section 114810.
